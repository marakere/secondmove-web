Why this exists

“The scoresheet holds the mistakes and the magic. It just never gets asked. Because in this digital world, moving paper to digital is hard. You’re busy. They’re busy. And somehow it never happens. I built this for my son so it would. If one child gets the chance to learn from their game, every child deserves that chance. If your son, your daughter or you sees what they missed — or what they got brilliantly right — and carries that forward, that’s the blessing. That’s why SecondMove came to life.”

I pay for this myself. About 220 scans a month is what I can afford. Once the limit’s reached, come back tomorrow — it resets at midnight. No subscription. No upsell. Just the game.
