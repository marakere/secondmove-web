Why we built this

“There’s a particular feeling — rooting through the chess bag two weeks after a tournament, finding a crumpled scoresheet, knowing your kid played something brilliant in round four, and having no way to know what it was.”

I’m a chess parent. Not a grandmaster, not a software engineer doing this for a portfolio — just someone who has sat on cold plastic chairs in school gymnasiums, watching my child think for twenty minutes over a single move, and feeling the quiet pride of that moment.

Chess teaches children something rare: how to sit with a hard problem and not flinch. Every game my child plays is a chapter in that education. I wanted to keep those chapters.

The problem is the scoresheet. Handwritten, smudged, sometimes barely legible — and completely useless to any software on earth. I tried the options. They all assumed you had a clean scan, a steady hand, or a subscription to justify the friction. None of them felt like they were made for the person in the parking lot after a Saturday tournament, trying to quickly photograph a scoresheet before the drive home.

So I built SecondMove. It uses Google’s Gemini AI to read even messy handwriting, validates every move against the rules of chess, and gives you a PGN you can load into Lichess or any other tool. It takes about fifteen seconds. It works offline once installed. It costs nothing.

This is our app. For chess families. Built with love and a deep appreciation for the crumpled scoresheet.

How it works

From photo to PGN
in three steps

1
📷

Photograph the scoresheet

Open SecondMove and tap the camera. Take a photo of any handwritten chess scoresheet — smudged, tilted, coffee-stained, it doesn’t matter. PDFs work too. The image stays on your device.

2
🧠

AI reads and validates every move

Gemini 2.5 AI reads the handwriting and extracts every move. Then chess.js validates the full game against legal chess rules — catching misreads before you ever see them. Uncertain moves are flagged so you can review and correct.

3
♕

Export or study

Copy the PGN to your clipboard, import directly into Lichess for analysis, or save it for later. Your child’s game is now preserved, searchable, and ready to be studied with any chess tool on earth.

Install SecondMove

Add it to your home screen

SecondMove is a Progressive Web App — it installs directly from your browser, with no App Store required. It loads fast, works offline, and feels like a native app.


iPhone & iPad
Safari required
  1. 1 Open SecondMove in Safari (not Chrome)
  2. 2 Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen ↗
  3. 3 Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  4. 4 Tap Add in the top-right corner
  5. 5 SecondMove now lives on your home screen ♛
🧛
Android
Chrome recommended
  1. 1 Open SecondMove in Chrome
  2. 2 Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner
  3. 3 Tap Add to Home screen
  4. 4 Tap Add to confirm
  5. 5 The app icon appears on your home screen ♛
Already on your laptop? SecondMove works in any modern browser too — just visit YOUR_APP_URL and start scanning. No installation required.

Your photos stay private

Images are sent to Google’s AI for OCR and immediately discarded. Nothing is stored. No user accounts. No tracking.

🎓

Free because it should be

Every chess family should be able to preserve their games. SecondMove is free to use. No paywalls, no subscriptions, no trials.

💪

Honest about its limits

AI isn’t perfect. SecondMove tells you when it’s uncertain and lets you review flagged moves before exporting.
