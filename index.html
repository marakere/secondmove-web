Every game your child plays holds a strength, an opportunity to grow, a pattern waiting to be learned.

SecondMove turns the handwritten scoresheet into a growth tool. Every move gets analysed, every pattern gets spotted, and nothing that happened on that board goes to waste.

Open SecondMove → See how it works

Free. No account needed. Works on any phone. For parents, coaches, players. Anyone with a scoresheet.

Why this exists

“After every game, I’d look at my son’s scoresheet and think: if we just go through this together, he’ll understand what happened on that board. But life doesn’t pause. Three days later, he can’t remember why he played that move, the notation is half-wrong, and the window is gone. I built SecondMove because I know exactly what that scoresheet is worth.”

This is our app. Built for chess players, their parents, and their coaches by someone who refused to let that window close.
