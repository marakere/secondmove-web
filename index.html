Why we built this

“You’re in the car park after the tournament. Your child is quiet — they know move 34 cost them the game. You want to help. But the scoresheet is in the bag, and even if you could read it, you wouldn’t know where to start. That drive home is a coaching moment that never happened.”

Chess improvement lives in the details. Not in how many games you play — in how closely you look at the ones you’ve already played. Are they falling into the same trap on move 12, tournament after tournament? Did they find a brilliant combination in round three that even they didn’t realise was brilliant? That’s where the real learning is.

But to see any of that, the moves have to get off paper and into a tool that can actually show you the board. Lichess. Chess.com. A coach’s laptop. Anything. And that one step — entering 40, 60, 80 handwritten moves by hand — is where it all falls apart. Kids miswrite notation. Parents can’t read it. One wrong entry breaks the whole game. So it just doesn’t happen. The notebook goes back in the bag. The lesson stays unlearned.

SecondMove is just that one step. Photograph the scoresheet — messy, smudged, tilted, doesn’t matter — and every move is online in seconds. Load it into Lichess. Replay it with your child on the drive home. Share it with the coach before the next session. Build a library of every game they’ve ever played and start to see the patterns that no single game can show you.

This isn’t just for chess parents. If you’re a coach who wants the full game without the transcription pain, this is for you. If you’re a player who keeps your own notation and wants it digital, this is for you. If you have a paper scoresheet and want it on a screen — whoever you are — this is for you.

This is our app. Built for the game. Not for a market.

How it works

Paper to Lichess.
Three steps. Fifteen seconds.

1
📷

Photograph the scoresheet

Open SecondMove and point your camera at the notation sheet. Messy handwriting, coffee stains, crooked columns — it handles all of it. PDFs work too. Your image never leaves your device.

2
🧠

Every move is read and checked

AI reads every move — all 40, 60, 80 of them. Then each one is validated against the actual rules of chess, catching misreads automatically. Anything uncertain is flagged so you can confirm it on the board right there in the app.

3
♕

Open it. Analyse it. Learn from it.

Load the game straight into Lichess or Chess.com. Replay every move with your child. Share it with the coach. Find the moment it turned. Spot the pattern that keeps coming back. Save it — every game, building into a record of how far they’ve come.

Install SecondMove

Add it to your home screen

SecondMove is a Progressive Web App — it installs directly from your browser, with no App Store required. It loads fast, works offline, and feels like a native app.


iPhone & iPad
Safari required
  1. 1 Open SecondMove in Safari (not Chrome)
  2. 2 Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen ↗
  3. 3 Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  4. 4 Tap Add in the top-right corner
  5. 5 SecondMove now lives on your home screen ♛
🧛
Android
Chrome recommended
  1. 1 Open SecondMove in Chrome
  2. 2 Tap the ⋮ menu in the top-right corner
  3. 3 Tap Add to Home screen
  4. 4 Tap Add to confirm
  5. 5 The app icon appears on your home screen ♛
Already on your laptop? SecondMove works in any modern browser too — just visit YOUR_APP_URL and start scanning. No installation required.
